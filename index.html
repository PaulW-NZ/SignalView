About Signal Analyzer

Welcome to Signal Analyzer!

This is a powerful tool for analyzing audio signals directly in your browser. You can load WAV or raw audio files and visualize them as a spectrogram or waveform.

Features:

  • Interactive spectrogram and waveform visualizations.
  • Customizable FFT parameters, color schemes, and grid controls.
  • Zooming, panning, and selection capabilities.
  • Detailed power spectrum analysis of selections.

How to Use:

  1. Click the "Choose File" button to load an audio file.
  2. Select the desired display type (spectrogram or waveform).
  3. Adjust the parameters in the control panels to customize the analysis.
  4. Click and drag on the visualization to make a selection.
  5. Right-click on a selection or marker to open the context menu for more options.
